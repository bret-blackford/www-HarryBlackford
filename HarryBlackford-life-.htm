  When Harry Arnold Blackford was born on December 21, 1914, in Illinois, his father, George, was 42 and his mother, Stella, was 34. He married Neva Louise Crackel on September 25, 1937. They had three children during their marriage. He died on February 4, 2001, in Fairfield, Illinois, at the age of 86.  
   
  When Neva Louise Crackel was born on September 26, 1915, in Edwards, Illinois, her father, John, was 22, and her mother, Myrtle, was 20. She married Harry Arnold Blackford on September 25, 1937. They had three children during their marriage. She died on February 3, 1987, in Wayne, Illinois, at the age of 71.
